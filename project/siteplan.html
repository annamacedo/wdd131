Overview

Site name: Gooday

This name represents a social networking site for people who exercise and eat well. The name alludes to "Good Day" suggesting that those who maintain a healthy lifestyle live better.

Purpose

Gooday aims to serve as a comprehensive social platform for fitness enthusiasts, offering a variety of services and information. It provides a hub where users can connect with like-minded individuals, discover groups dedicated to different sports activities, explore healthy dining options, and track friends' fitness achievements through an interactive timeline.

Scenarios

  • Question: I'm new to Gooday and interested in finding local groups for long-distance running. How can I find and connect with groups dedicated to this sport in my area?
  • Question: I'm struggling to maintain my exercise routine and would like a way to track my progress and stay motivated. How can Gooday's personalized timeline help me see my friends' achievements and keep me engaged with my own fitness plan?
  • Quesion: I'm looking for restaurants that offer healthy options near my location. How can I use Gooday to discover restaurant recommendations that align with my healthy lifestyle?
